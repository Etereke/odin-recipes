Grilled cheese sandwich recipe

Description

Sandwich made out of cheese, grilled to perfection. One of the simplest pleasures of life

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Sandwich griller

Steps

  1. Take a piece of bread and add a thin layer of butter on it
  2. Put some cheese on it
  3. Put another piece of bread on it
  4. Place it into the sandwich griller and wait until it finishes
  5. Take it out and enjoy!
